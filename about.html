Abdelrahman Hossam Host of X Talks

Abdelrahman Hossam is a Mass Communication graduate from Cairo University, Radio & Television Department (Class of 2024), with a passion for storytelling, media production, and meaningful conversations. Throughout his journey, he has gained experience in both radio and video hosting, developing strong communication and presentation skills across different media platforms. Today, he hosts X Talks Podcast, a project that represents a major milestone in his professional career and reflects his commitment to creating engaging and impactful content.

Beyond hosting, Abdelrahman works as a Voice Over Artist, Sound Engineer, and Content Creator. His experience in audio production and content development allows him to combine creativity with technical expertise, delivering high-quality media experiences from concept to final production. Through every project, he aims to create content that informs, inspires, and connects with audiences in an authentic and memorable way.
