# X Talks Podcast Website

A premium, production-ready, editorial-style website built for **X Talks** — a podcast about modern masculinity, society, and personal growth.

---

## 🎙️ About the Podcast
* **Host:** Abdelrahman Hossam
* **Tagline:** *"X Talks presented by Abdelrahman Hossam"*
* **Description:**  
  *X Talks is a podcast that explores the realities, challenges, and perceptions surrounding men in today's society. Through insightful conversations with directors, lawyers, life coaches, content creators, and industry experts, we discuss topics ranging from media representation and family law to relationships, self-awareness, mental health, and personal growth. Our goal is to encourage meaningful dialogue, challenge stereotypes, and promote a deeper understanding of the issues that shape modern life.*

---

## ✨ Features & Design Aesthetic

1. **Brand Identity:** High-contrast color palette utilizing a dominant white background (#FFFFFF) with a deep, vibrant royal blue (#1A2EFF) as the primary accent.
2. **Editorial Typography:** Google Fonts pairing using **Syne** (bold, geometric, ultra-modern) for primary headings, **Bebas Neue** (strong, condensed) for numbers and badges, and **Plus Jakarta Sans** (clean, readable) for body text and navigation.
3. **Animated Waveform:** A custom, CSS-animated audio waveform consisting of 5 vertical bars that bounce using a keyframe animation in the hero section.
4. **Watermark Watermark:** A giant, subtle, almost invisible background "X" watermark behind the hero content for modern media depth.
5. **Grain Overlay:** A tactile noise texture overlay created using high-performance inline SVG fractal noise.
6. **Card Hover Actions:** Episode cards lift and cast deep drop shadows on hover, displaying a thin blue progress bar filling from left to right at the bottom of the cover wrapper.
7. **Client-Side Filtering:** The episodes page features a real-time instant search bar and guest badges to filter episodes instantly.
8. **Responsive Layout:** Fully mobile-friendly and responsive, adapting cleanly from desktop grids to mobile stacks.
