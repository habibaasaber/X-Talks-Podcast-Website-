X Talks Podcast

Exploring Modern Masculinity, Society, and Personal Growth

X Talks is a podcast that explores the realities, challenges, and perceptions surrounding men in today's society. Through insightful conversations with directors, lawyers, life coaches, content creators, and industry experts, we discuss topics ranging from media representation and family law to relationships, self-awareness, mental health, and personal growth. Our goal is to encourage meaningful dialogue, challenge stereotypes, and promote a deeper understanding of the issues that shape modern life.
